Dynasty Rush

About the Game

Dynasty Rush is an exhilarating strategy-based action game set in ancient China, where players lead legendary warlords to conquer rival dynasties. Commanding an army, players engage in tactical battles, utilize unique abilities of different generals, and manage resources to rise to power. With a deep story and vibrant historical world, Dynasty Rush offers a thrilling mix of action and strategy.

Key Features

  • Epic battles featuring historical generals
  • Strategic resource management and base-building
  • Multiple game modes, including campaigns and multiplayer
  • Dynamic weather and day-night cycle affecting gameplay
  • Vivid visuals with ancient Chinese art and architecture
