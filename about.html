About Us

We Are the Most Prominent Software Provider

At Gaming Den, we strive to deliver cutting-edge technology and unparalleled gaming experiences. Our mission is to lead the marketplace with innovative solutions and a dedication to excellence.

Our Story

Founding Story

Founded in 2018 in Pakistan, Gaming Den began as a passion project for three lifelong friends and gaming enthusiasts. Their shared dream was to create innovative games that pushed the boundaries of storytelling and gameplay.

Milestones and Achievements

2018: Gaming Den released its first indie game, Legend Hero, a fast-paced 2D shooter.
2020: Their breakthrough came with Mobile Legends, an open-world RPG that became a massive hit.
2022: Gaming Den introduced Arcadia VR, their first virtual reality game.
2023: The company launched Jade Realm, a MOBA game.

Growth and Expansion

Starting as a small studio with just 8 developers, Gaming Den has grown into a global gaming powerhouse with over 400 team members across four offices worldwide.

Challenges and Resilience

In 2021, the company faced a major challenge with the delayed launch of Starforge Chronicles. Despite initial backlash, the team remained transparent and delivered an exceptional gaming experience.

Community and Culture

Gaming Den takes pride in its vibrant and inclusive gaming community, ensuring its games reflect a wide range of cultures and perspectives.

Current Focus and Future Plans

Gaming Den continues to innovate with their latest title, Eclipse Odyssey, set to launch in late 2025. Their mission remains to create unforgettable adventures worldwide.
